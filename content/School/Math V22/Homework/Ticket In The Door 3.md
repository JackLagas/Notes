# Question 1
Fill in the blanks.
$$
\text{Let M =}
\left [{\begin{array}{cc}
1 & 3 \\
-10 & -8 \\
-2 & 4 \\
-4 & -1 \\
\end{array}}\right]
$$
This is a \_\_\_ x \_\_\_ matrix, where m<sub>21</sub> = \_\_\_

## Answer:
This is a 4x2 matrix, where m<sub>21</sub> = -10
