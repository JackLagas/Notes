# Question 1
Write the augmented coefficient matrix corresponding to the system:
$$
\begin{cases}
& -8x + 10 = -5y \\
& -5x -9y = -8 \\
& -x + 10 = 0 \\
\end{cases}
$$
### Answer:
$$
\left[{ \begin{array}{cc}
-8 & 5 & -10 \\
-5 & -9 & -8 \\
-1 & 0 & -10
\end{array}} \right]
$$
